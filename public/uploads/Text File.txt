Creamy Lemon Pasta
by BARBARA KAFKA

INGREDIENTS
12 ounces wide egg noodles
 Zest and juice of 2 lemons; zest cut in very thin strips 1 inch long
1 cup heavy cream
 Kosher salt, to taste
 Lots of freshly ground black pepper
